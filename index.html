Unfiltered was founded by professional spray tan artists who were tired of compromising. Too patchy. Too orange. Too unpredictable. So they built something better — formulations developed with cosmetic scientists, tested in high-volume salons, and refined until every result was worth showing off.

Today, Unfiltered is trusted by leading beauty professionals across the United States and is now making its mark in Europe — starting with Poland. Every product is designed with one purpose: to deliver a flawless, natural-looking tan that your clients will come back for.
